  1. sovreignty

      • create 1824 bureau fo indians affairs to maintian peace
  2. strategies for acquiring valuable tribal land
    • removal
      • killing native amer, scalping
      • indian removal act 1830
        • relocate all eastern tribes across mississippi river, trail of tears
      • annex land
    • assimilation
      • boarding school
      • allotment act of 1887
        • bypass tribal leaders, make native amer land owners (land not communal)
