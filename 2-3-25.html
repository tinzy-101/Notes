Absolute Cell Address

  • \(A\)5
  • put something that's changing at the top of the spreadshet as a parameter
  • is a cell reference that remains the same even when formula is copied or moved
  • e.g. want the formula B21**C32 to iterate down
    • only want B21 to iterate down a column, but want C32 to stay the same
    • formula: B21**\(C\)32
    • C32 will stay the same for each iteration
