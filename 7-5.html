Achasan Planning Notes:
Transportation: 
	Tina: about 27 min, green line 2 to 왕십리, then purple line 5 to 아차산, exit 2
	Laryssa: not coming
	
1. Make left after exit 2, at the end turn right and might see kendo dojo
2. follow road bending left
3. turn left onto Jayang-ro, will see mountain
4. walk towards mountain, fork right onto Jayang-ro 50-gil, will pass by restaurant, cafe, hiking gear shops
5. keep straight up til road turns left in from of 诗華永山嵯峨
6. Follow road to the left
7. Will get to some green steps that are start of trail (15 min walk from station)
8. Keep walking up, will pass a pavilion, good views, want to keep heading north
9. Will pass by many forts. After fort 4, the biggest fort will get fork in the path
10. Take wooden steps going down, NOT dirt road
11. Go down, pass by helipad marked by stone H.
12. Once reaching the clearing, take left from clearing, and will get to another clearing + open air gym
13. After walking up, will get to top of Yongmasan
14. From Yongmasan, can descend to Yongma Station. 40 min, and gets steep
15. Will pass by Yongmasan Fort 7.
16. Will walk past apartment buildings then get to main road, Rongmasan ro.
17. Turn left, walk 10 min to Yongmasan station
18. Can also check out Yongma waterfalls if have time!

Info: https://4corners7seas.com/asia/korea/hiking-in-seoul/achasan/

Bukhansan Planning Notes:
Transportation: 

Option 1: 
Better for get off at Ui Ranger Station on the east --> get off at Bukhansan Ui Station exit 2,
make a left and will see a CU. Hike up for 40 min (no bus), need to get to the ranger station
Will eventually get to Doseonsa Temple (halfway up the mountain), where trail officially starts
Recommended to go see Doseonsa temple, then go back to parking lot and look for 백운대, tallest 
peak on the mountain range. 

Things that you'll pass by:
1. police mountain rescue hut
2. insubond, one of Bukhansan's 3 horns
3. stone shelter
4. wimun gate (steep climb before top)

All trails route: https://www.alltrails.com/explore/trail/south-korea/seoul/bukhansan-ui-station-bukhansan-mountain-loop?mobileMap=false&ref=sidebar-static-map

Option 2:
Line 3 to Gupabal station, exit 1 to bus stop, take buss # 704 to 북한산성 bus station.
Will be lots of restaurants and gear shops. Find park headquarters for maps and trailhead.
Will start by small river, but will eventually leave river and trail gets steep.

Things you'll pass by:
1. river, then steep forest only
2. long flight of wooden steps up to Wimun gate, then turn left for final section
3. leave the forest, and climb up granite slabs at summit

If doing BOTH options:
Start from Gupabal and finish at Ui, might be too much tho


Super helpful guidebook: https://english.visitseoul.net/map-guide-book
