German Americans

  1. settlement patterns
    • late 1700s religious dissenters from germany to pennsylvania and move west
    • 1830-1890 1/4 immigrarnts in US
    • 1880s 10% pop of US
  2. 21th century german america
    • 1901 german-american national alliance
    • fought against germany and distanced from homeland alliances in WW1/WW2
  3. irish americans
    • colonial period: 2nd largest group behind english
    • diversity based on time of entry, settlement area, religion
    • mostly peasants
    • fled from certain death (1845 potato crop famine 1845)
    • mob violence against catholics
  4. becoming white
    • employers mixed immigrant groups to prevent unified action from laborers
    • experienced slow advancement, more "white" than "immigrant"
    • distanced from lower class
    • lace curtain vs shanty irish
    • 2nd largest ancestry in US, slight immigration now
