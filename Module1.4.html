The importance of fair business decisions

  1. business analytics
    • issue: topic or subject to investigate
    • question: designed to discover info
    • problem: obstacle/complication to work out
    • business task: question or problem data analysis answers for business
  2. data and fairness
    • fairness: analysis doesnt create or reinforce bias
    • systems that are fair and inclusive
    • sometimes conclusions are true AND unfair
      • e.g. male dominated fields, data concludes that need to hire more men
      • doesnt check assumptions or outside factors
    • need to factor in social context --> need to consider throughout WHOLE process
  3. best practices
    • consdier all available data
      • cant ignore nonrelevant data, or stuff that doesnt align with epectations
    • identify surrounding factors
    • include self-reported data
      • self-reporting = participant provide info about self
      • prevents observer bias
    • effectively use oversampling
      • oversampling = increase sample size of nondominant groups in population
      • so can better address imbalanced datasets
    • thingk about fairness from beginning to end (collection, cleaning, processing, analysis)
  4. need to keep n mind how are people actually impacted by data at the end of the day
    • responsible for people reflected in data
    • think about keeping data protected
    • need to give consumers more control over their data, consent

  1. data analyst vs scientist
    • data analyst = use existing tools to solve problems, help stakeholders make better decisions
      • database queries, visualization, dashboards, reports, spreadsheets
    • data scientist = invent new tools and models, collect new data, analyze and interpret complex data to make business predictions
      • stats, ML, deep learning, data optimization, programming
