Craft effective questions

  1. SMART questions
    • better to ask more questions
    • some qs better than others
    • leading question: answer is like predetermined
    • close-ended question: answered w/ yes or no (helpful!)
    • open-ended question: vague and lacks context (whats your fav ice cream)
    • SMART
      • specific
        • simple, significant, focuses on a single topic or few closely related ideas
      • measurable
        • can be quantified and assessed
      • actionable
        • encourage change
      • relevant
        • matter, are important and have significance to problem ur trying to solve
      • time bound
        • specify time period to be studied
    • fairness
      • dont add "aren't they, aren't you" to end of a quesiton bc it is leading
  2. data opens doors
    • many diff career paths
    • e.g. data analyst
      • work with data engineer and data scientist
      • engineers build data pipelines
      • analysts provide clean and actionable data
      • data scientists turn to ML models + statistical inferences
